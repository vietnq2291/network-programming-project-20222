Commands:
Requests: 
    Login:
        R L <username> <password>

    Register:
        R S <username> <password> <display_name>
    Update:
        Change name:
            R U N <new_name>
        Change password:
            R U P <new_password>
    Logout:
        R X
    Create new chat (for group chat or private chat):
        R C P <other_user_id>
        R C G <group_name> <number of members> <member_1_user_id> <member_2_user_id> ...
Chat:
    C G <chat_id> <message>
    C P <chat_id> <message>
